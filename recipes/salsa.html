Making Salsa Made Easy

Experience the vibrant flavors of this refreshing mango and avocado salsa, a perfect blend of sweet, tangy, and creamy textures. This versatile dish can be served as a delicious appetizer with tortilla chips or as a delightful accompaniment to grilled chicken, fish, or tacos. Bursting with bright colors and packed with nutrients, this mango and avocado salsa will become a favorite for your summer gatherings or simply for a healthy and flavorful snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeds removed and minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced mangoes, avocados, chopped red onion, and minced jalapeño.
  2. Add the chopped cilantro, lime juice, and extra-virgin olive oil. Gently toss the ingredients together to ensure they are well combined.
  3. Season the salsa with salt and pepper, adjusting to taste.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid, and refrigerate the salsa for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the mango and avocado salsa chilled with tortilla chips, or as a side dish to your favorite grilled meats or tacos. Enjoy the delightful burst of flavors in every bite!
