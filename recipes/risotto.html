Making Risotto Made Easy

Indulge in this mouthwatering creamy spinach and mushroom risotto, a luxurious Italian classic with a twist. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms pair perfectly with the savory spinach and creamy Arborio rice, creating an irresistibly rich and hearty dish. Topped with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, this risotto is perfect for a cozy night in or an elegant dinner party.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms (cremini or button)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps:

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the vegetable broth over low heat, keeping it warm.
  2. In a large, deep skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute, stirring to avoid burning.
  4. Stir in the Arborio rice, ensuring it is well-coated with the oil, onion, and garlic mixture. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Gradually add the warm vegetable broth to the rice, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously. Allow each ladle of broth to be absorbed before adding the next one. This process should take about 20-25 minutes.
  6. In a separate skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are tender and release their juices, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Once the rice has absorbed all the broth and reached a creamy consistency, stir in the cooked mushrooms and chopped spinach. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the spinach has wilted.
  8. Remove the risotto from the heat, and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy your delicious creamy spinach and mushroom risotto!
